Neil Keegan

Co-Managing Partner, CEO

Neil has deep investment expertise in alternative assets, including private equity, venture capital, family office, and real estate. Neil previously founded his own investment company, Keegan Capital Management (KCM). During his tenure, he spearheaded eight transactions, notably Palantir and Voyager Space Holdings. Prior to KCM, he was the CEO and Chief Investment Officer for Roanoke Capital Management (RCM) for eleven years, a single-family office based out of Los Angeles, CA. Neil also spent seven years at Goldman, Sachs & Co in their Private Wealth Management and Prime Brokerage/Hedge Funds divisions.

  • Neil’s successes include a successful, direct co-investment in SpaceX that has produced an IRR of approximately 35% to date.
  • He was also an early adopter of the family office “profits interest structure,” and brings unique insights having been an integral part in establishing this structure and administering it for RCM.

As a veteran, he served for six years on active duty as a Surface Warfare Officer in the United States Navy and ran the Command, Seamanship, and Navigation, Training Squadron for the Brigade of Midshipmen as a Naval Leadership instructor.

Neil is a proud graduate of Johns Hopkins University School of Advanced International Studies (SAIS) with a MA in International Relations with a concentration in International Finance. He currently resides in Chevy Chase, MD with his twin daughters.

He is a Board Member of Yellow Ribbon Fund, a charitable organization that provides support to injured service people and their families upon return from the battlefield (Home – Yellow Ribbon Fund). Neil is also an active alumnus and Trustee of the U.S. Naval Academy Athletic & Scholarship Program. He also holds his Series 7 license and is a Registered Representative with Roberts & Ryan, a Service-Disabled Broker-Dealer (Service-Disabled Veteran-Owned Broker-Dealer | Roberts & Ryan (Roberts-ryan.com)).
